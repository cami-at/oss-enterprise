Wedges with vanilla sauße

  • Time: 2h 35min
  • Serves: 15 portions
Ingredients Quantity Image
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Dry yeast
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla sugar
  • Egg(s)
  • Pinch(s) of salt
  • Melted butter
  • 200 ml
  • 75 g
  • 500 g
  • 1 packet
  • 75 g
  • 1 packet
  • 1
  • 1
  • 50 g

Step 1: Slowly heat the milk and butter on the stove until the butter has melted.

Step 2: Mix the flour, salt, yeast, sugar and vanilla sugar together.

Step 3: Add the butter-milk and an egg and knead everything well with the dough hook of a hand mixer until the dough loosens from the edge of the bowl.

Step 4: Cover and let rise for about 1 hour.

Step 5: Knead the dough briefly on a floured worktop, shape into a roll and cut into approx. 15 slices.

Step 6: Place 1 teaspoon of jam in the middle and roll the dough pieces into balls.

Step 7: Grease a springform pan or a baking dish and place the dough balls next to each other. Let rise for another 20 minutes.

Step 8: Buchteln go into the oven, pour melted butter over them. Bake at 180 ° C top / bottom heat for approx. 25 minutes.

Pancakes

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 4 portions
Ingredients Quantity Image
  • Flour
  • Milk or water
  • Egg(s)
  • Salt
  • some sugar
  • Butter for frying


  • Option 1:
  • strawberries
  • Cognac
  • Powdered sugar

  • Option 2:
  • Apricot jam
  • Lighlty roasted almonds
  • Powdered sugar
  • 12 eating spoons
  • 500 ml
  • 4
  • 2 pinches
  • a bit (depends on your taste)
  • 8 eating spoons

  • 500 g
  • a bit of
  • to sprinkle


  • 200 g
  • a bunch of
  • to sprinkle

Step 1: Put the flour in a bowl, add the milk or mineral water and stir with the whisk to a smooth mass. Add the eggs to the batter. Salt a pinch of salt and add sugar to taste and beat everything well. The dough must be thick. Then let it rest for 30 minutes.

Step 2: Heat the fat in a pan with a spoon, add the pancake batter and let it flow evenly. Bake the pancakes until golden and turn them with a wide spatula. Add a little fat and bake until golden. Roll up the finished pancakes or place them on top of each other and keep them warm on a preheated plate in the oven until all of the pancakes are fried.

Optional step(s): For the strawberry pancakes, prepare a pancake batter with sugar according to the basic recipe. Puree 250 g of washed strawberries in a blender and flavor with sugar and a little cognac. Brush the baked pancakes with the strawberry puree, roll up, place on preheated plates and sprinkle with a little sugar. Serve immediately.

For the Parisian pancakes, prepare a pancake batter with sugar according to the basic recipe and bake thin pancakes from it. Brush each pancake with apricot jam, sprinkle with lightly roasted, chopped almonds, fold and dust with powdered sugar.

Tip: For a salty dough, suitable for hearty side dishes or fillings, replace milk / mineral water with beer and do not add sugar to the dough.
